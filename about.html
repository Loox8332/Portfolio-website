About me

Current second-year mechanical and electrical engineering student at the University of Bristol. I spend my time using my problem solving and engineering skills to work on advanced personal projects alongside my degree. My academic work combines a strong foundation in mechanical design, electronics, and systems integration, which is relevant for my passion in fields such as robotics, renewable energy solutions, and sustainable product development.

In addition to my studies, I enjoy working on challenging projects to enhance my practical understanding of my interests. I am currently working on a 6 dof manipulator using ros to model its motion, teaching myself everything as I go. All my projects are detailed on my website.

Throughout my learning, both through my degree and out of it, I have gained advanced coding knowledge in Python, Java, C, and more. I have also learnt how to design circuits and mechanical components using a variety of cad packages. This knowledge has helped me succeed in my degree and allowed me to work more collaboratively on group design projects, where I have refined my problem solving and communications skills.

I am excited to gain more experience in industry through internships and placement opportunities in which I can share my enthusiasm and innovation deliver meaningful impact. I aspire to work sustainably and effectively to provide engineering solutions for a better, more advanced future.
