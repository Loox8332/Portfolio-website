Currently, I have been working on a monocular SLAM mapper that recreates its environment digitally using a single RGB camera. This project has already highlighted my advanced practical design skills in both software and hardware applications. Royal Mint research project

As part of a nominated project with The Royal Mint, I led a research driven team initiative with a focus on historical innovation that rediscovered a previously lost 17th century coin manufacturing method. Key highlights of my role included authoring a detailed research article uncovering and re-evaluating evidence on the evolution of raised edge coin manufacturing, collaborating with scholars, and exploring new research territories for The Royal Mint. Alongside this I guided a team of five in the design and creation of a functional prototype of this method, that was once a pivotal tool in British coinage production. This project honed my research skills and strengthened my leadership skills, historical investigation, and collaborative innovation.
